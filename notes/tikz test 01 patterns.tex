\section*{Pattern Demonstrations}

\subsection*{primeddots Pattern}

The \texttt{primeddots} pattern creates evenly-spaced dots:

\begin{center}
\tz{
  \fill[primedregion=blue!20] (0,0) rectangle (3,2);
  \draw[thick] (0,0) rectangle (3,2);
  \node at (1.5,1) {primeddots};
}
\end{center}

\subsection*{primeddots2 Pattern}

\subsection*{Combined Patterns}

Multiple regions with different patterns:

\begin{center}
\tz[scale=0.8]{
  \fill[primedregion=blue!15] (0,0) circle (1);
  \fill[boxregion=red!15] (2.5,0) circle (1);
  \fill[starregion=green!15] (5,0) circle (1);
  \fill[diagregion=orange!15] (1.25,1.8) circle (1);
  \fill[hlineregion=purple!15] (3.75,1.8) circle (1);

  \node at (0,0) {dots};
  \node at (2.5,0) {box};
  \node at (5,0) {star};
  \node at (1.25,1.8) {diag};
  \node at (3.75,1.8) {hlines};
}
\end{center}
