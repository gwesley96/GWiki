3-manifold

Topics: low-dimensional-topology, 3-manifolds
Last modified: 2025-12-09

\begin{framedidea} Low-dimensional topology packages 3-manifolds via surgery: starting from $S^3$, perform Dehn surgery along framed links to obtain every orientable 3-manifold. \end{framedidea}

By Dehn surgery, every orientable manifold 3-manifold can be presented as a pair $(K,r)$ where $K$ is a knot in $S^{3}$ and $r=p/q\in\mathbb{Q}\cup\{\infty\}$ (called the \textbf{surgery coefficient}).
