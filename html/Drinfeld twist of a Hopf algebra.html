Drinfeld twist of a Hopf algebra

Last modified: 2025-12-08 18:37
Created: 2025-12-09

A Drinfeld twist $A^{J}$ by some $J$ (??) of a hopf-algebra $H$ is a Hopf algebra $H^{J}$ that twist the coalgebra structure but not the algebra structure in that $H^{J}\cong H$ as Hopf algebras.
