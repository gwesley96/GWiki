Example. In a Presemisimple 2-Category, any two objects $a$ and $b$ in the same connected component condense onto each other: from a 1-morphism $f\colon a\to b$, the existence of adjoints means that there is an $f^{R}\colon b\to a$, and one can show that $(f,f^{R})$ is part of the data of a condensation $\,\longrightarrow\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\hookleftarrow{\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\color{white}\blacksquare\color{black}}\,\,b\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!\!a\,\,\,\,\,\,\,\,\,\,\,\,$.
